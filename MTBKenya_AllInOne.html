About This NTSA Driving Theory Quiz

The MTB Kenya NTSA Driving Theory Quiz is a free online practice exam designed to help Kenyan drivers and boda boda riders pass the official NTSA (National Transport and Safety Authority) driving theory test on their first attempt.

What Does This Quiz Cover?

  • 🛑 Kenya Road Signs — Regulatory, Warning, Informatory
  • 🚦 Traffic Rules and Traffic Light Laws
  • 🏍️ Boda Boda (Motorcycle) Regulations and Helmet Laws
  • ⚡ Speed Limits — Urban, Highway, School Zones
  • 🍺 Drink Driving BAC Limits (Traffic Act Section 44)
  • 🅿️ Parking Rules and Road Markings
  • 📋 MTB (Model Town Board) Driving Test Rules
  • 🌧️ Hazard Awareness — Aquaplaning, Blind Spots, Following Distance

How to Pass the NTSA Driving Theory Test in Kenya

The NTSA driving theory test requires a minimum pass mark of 75%. The exam covers the Kenya Highway Code, road signs, traffic rules, and specific regulations for different vehicle classes including motorcycles (boda boda), PSVs, and private vehicles.

Practice daily with our 3 difficulty levels — Beginner (Road Signs), Intermediate (Traffic Rules), and Expert (Hazard Awareness & Boda-Boda Laws) — to build confidence and road knowledge.

Boda Boda Rules in Kenya — Key Facts

  • Both rider AND passenger must wear helmets (mandatory by law)
  • Maximum 1 passenger only on commercial boda boda
  • Reflective jacket/vest mandatory for both rider and passenger
  • Minimum engine capacity for commercial use: 100cc
  • Zero BAC tolerance for commercial motorcycle operators
